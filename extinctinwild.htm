Extinct in wild Species

This Page is about species that are extinct in the wild meaning you wont see them out and about only captured in Zoos, ISPCA or any places similar. I will link some places where you can see the animals that are extinct in the wild.

Some causes of Animal Extinction in the wild are Demographic and genetic phenomena. First of all, species with small populations face a greater risk of extinction.

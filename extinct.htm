Extinct Species

This page is about Animals that are extinct meaning they are not held captive anywhere and they are completely gone from the earth examples of some animals in the images below

Extinction is the termination of a kind of organism or of a group of kinds, usually a species. The moment of extinction is generally considered to be the death of the last individual of the species, although the capacity to breed and recover may have been lost before this point.

West African Black Rhinoceros The West African Black Rhinoceros was found in several countries towards the southeast region of Africa.
Baiji White Dolphin Baiji White Dolphin, also called the Chinese River Dolphin, can only be found in the Yangtze River in China.
Pyrenean Ibex One of four subspecies of the Spanish Ibex or Iberian Goat that was found in the Iberian Peninsula.
Stellers Sea Cow Named after George Steller, a naturalist who discovered the creature in 1741, Stellers Sea Cow was a large herbivorous mammal.
